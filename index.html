Introduction

Hi there! I'm Erin, a CPA-certified Accountant with a passion for numbers, finance, and unraveling insights hidden within data. As an experienced accountant, I've always been fascinated by the stories numbers can tell. However, my curiosity extends beyond traditional accounting methods. I'm on a journey to delve deeper into the world of data analysis, leveraging tools like Python, SQL, and Tableau to unlock new perspectives and opportunities.

With a strong foundation in accounting principles and a thirst for learning, I'm actively honing my skills in Python programming and SQL querying during my spare time. My goal? To bridge the gap between finance and data analysis, bringing a unique perspective to the table and driving informed decision-making through data-driven insights.

Here on my personal website, you'll find a collection of my projects where I explore the intersection of finance and data analysis. From financial forecasting models to interactive dashboards visualizing market trends, each project reflects my dedication to mastering the art of data storytelling.
